Our Story

From a taco truck in East Salinas to a cafecito of our own

Rooted in Family & Culture

Paloma's Cafecito was born from a simple truth: in a Mexican household, coffee is always there — for every morning, every celebration, every hard day. Owner Jennifer Perez Garcia grew up watching her mother build something from nothing, running the beloved Lonchera La Paloma food trucks in East Salinas since Jennifer was 13 years old.

That hustle, that love, and that spirit of familia is woven into every cup we serve. Jennifer and her partner Bryan Hernandez turned a home-based dream into a real storefront — and they did it with the support of their whole family every step of the way.

"I grew up in a Mexican household where we always had coffee for any occasion." — Jennifer Perez Garcia
